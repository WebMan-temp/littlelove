# Friend Photos

Place your photos here and update references in `lib/constants.ts`:

- `friend/photo1.jpg`
- `friend/photo2.jpg`
- `friend/photo3.jpg`
- `friend/photo4.jpg`
- `friend/photo5.jpg`
- `friend/photo6.jpg`

Tip: Use JPG or PNG. Large images will be optimized by Next.js automatically.

Extras for new features:

- `friend/special.jpg` — a very special image revealed by the top heart button.
- `friend/bg.mp3` — background music file. It plays when you press the floating “Play music” button, and also auto-starts when the secret image opens.
